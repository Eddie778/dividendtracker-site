Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a holding?
From the Holdings tab, tap the + button. Enter the ticker symbol, number of shares, cost basis, and purchase date. The app fetches current price and dividend data automatically.
How accurate are the dividend yields?
Yields are calculated from trailing-12-month dividend payments fetched from public market data. For consistent dividend payers (most blue-chip stocks and ETFs), yields are highly accurate. For variable-distribution funds (covered call ETFs, REITs), yields can fluctuate as recent payment history shifts.
How do I connect my brokerage account?
Go to Settings → Broker Sync → Connect Broker. You'll be redirected to SnapTrade's secure portal where you sign in to your broker. All connections are read-only — we cannot trade, transfer, or modify your account in any way.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All your portfolio data is stored on your device, optionally synced to your personal iCloud. We don't have user accounts, don't collect personal information, and don't use third-party analytics. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
What's included in Pro?
Dividend Tracker Pro unlocks unlimited holdings, all themes, broker sync, advanced insights (concentration analysis, blended yield, tax estimates, what-if simulator), and CSV import. Free users can track up to 5 holdings with the default theme.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Settings → Manage Subscription opens iOS's subscription manager. You can cancel anytime; Pro features remain active until the end of your current billing period. We don't process cancellations directly — Apple handles all subscription management.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are processed by Apple, not by us. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund. Apple typically approves refunds for accidental purchases or technical issues.
How do I import holdings from a CSV?
Settings → Data → Import CSV. The expected columns are: Symbol, Shares, Cost Basis, Purchase Date. A template is shown in the import sheet. You can export from most brokerages — see your broker's "Account Activity" or "Tax Documents" sections.
A specific stock is showing wrong dividend data — what should I do?
Dividend data comes from public market sources and can occasionally have errors, especially for newly-listed stocks, recent splits, or special distributions. Pull down on the Holdings tab to refresh. If a specific stock still looks wrong, email us with the ticker and we'll investigate.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. Your holdings and history are stored locally and viewable offline. Live prices and new dividend data require an internet connection.
