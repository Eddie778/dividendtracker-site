Privacy Policy

Your data stays
on your device.

No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. Built the way a finance app should be built.

Last updated: May 7, 2026
In Plain English

The short version

Dividend Tracker stores your portfolio data locally on your device. We don't collect personal information, don't use third-party analytics, and don't share data with advertisers. There are no user accounts to create.

What the app collects

Dividend Tracker collects only what's required for the app to function:

  • Holdings data — ticker symbols, shares, cost basis, purchase dates entered manually by you. Stored locally on your device. Optionally synced via Apple iCloud if iCloud is enabled in iOS settings.
  • Goals and preferences — income targets, theme selection, notification preferences. Stored locally.
  • Brokerage connection metadata — read-only access tokens stored securely in the iOS Keychain when you choose to connect a broker.
  • Apple subscription receipts — handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment details.

What the app does not collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or address
  • No third-party analytics — no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Segment, or Mixpanel
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data sold, rented, or shared with advertisers
  • No user accounts, sign-up flows, or login

Market data

Stock prices and dividend information are fetched from Finnhub through an anonymous proxy server. Each request contains only the ticker symbols requested (such as "VOO" or "QQQI"). These queries are not associated with any user identifier.

Brokerage connections (optional)

If you choose to connect a brokerage account, the connection is handled through SnapTrade — a regulated third-party service. The app:

  • Receives only read-only access to your account holdings and transactions
  • Cannot place trades, transfer money, or modify your account in any way
  • Stores the connection token securely in the iOS Keychain on your device only
  • Never sees your broker username or password — these are entered directly into SnapTrade's secure portal

Disconnect at any time from the Settings tab. SnapTrade's privacy practices: snaptrade.com/privacy

Subscriptions and payments

Pro subscriptions are processed via Apple's In-App Purchase system. The app receives:

  • Subscription status (active, expired, trial)
  • Anonymous receipt validation tokens used to verify purchases

The app never sees your credit card number, Apple ID, or billing address.

Children's privacy

Dividend Tracker is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect data from children.

Data retention and deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it:

  • Delete the app from your iPhone — all local data is removed
  • Disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings if iCloud-synced
  • Disconnect your broker connection in Settings → Broker Sync

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Significant changes will also be announced in-app.
